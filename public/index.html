Find answers to common questions about the Kenya Great Party

Membership

To become a member, you need to:

  • Fill out the membership application form on our website
  • Provide valid identification documents
  • Pay the required membership fee
  • Complete the verification process

Membership fees are as follows:

  • Ordinary Member: KSh 100/year
  • Youth Member (18-35): KSh 50/year
  • Student Member: KSh 25/year
  • Life Member: KSh 5,000 (one-time)

You can check your membership status by:

  • Visiting our membership status page
  • Entering your ID number and phone number
  • Contacting our membership office directly

To resign from the party, you can:

  • Submit a resignation request through our website
  • Visit any KGP office with your ID
  • Send a written resignation letter to our headquarters

Party Activities

As a member, you can participate in various ways:

  • Attend local branch meetings
  • Volunteer for party events and campaigns
  • Join specialized committees
  • Participate in policy discussions

Party meetings are held at different levels:

  • Local branch meetings: Monthly
  • County meetings: Quarterly
  • National meetings: Annually
  • Special meetings: As needed
