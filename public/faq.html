Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Kenya Great Party

Membership

To become a member, you need to:

  • Be a Kenyan citizen
  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Fill out the membership application form on our website
  • Provide valid identification documents
  • Agree to our party constitution and values

You can start your application by visiting our Join KGP page.

Membership fees are as follows:

  • Ordinary Member: KSh 100 per year
  • Youth Member (18-35 years): KSh 50 per year
  • Student Member: KSh 25 per year
  • Life Member: KSh 5,000 (one-time payment)

Payment can be made through M-Pesa, bank transfer, or at our party offices.

You can check your membership status by:

  • Logging into your account on our website
  • Calling our membership hotline: 0700 000 000
  • Visiting any of our party offices
  • Sending an email to membership@kenyagreatparty.org

To resign from the party, you can:

  • Submit a written resignation letter to the party secretary
  • Use our online resignation form (coming soon)
  • Visit any party office and fill out a resignation form

Your resignation will be processed within 30 days of receipt.

Party Activities

As a member, you can participate in various ways:

  • Attend party meetings and conventions
  • Join party committees and working groups
  • Volunteer for party events and campaigns
  • Contribute to policy development
  • Run for party positions in internal elections

Check our News section for upcoming events and activities.

Party meetings are held at different levels:

  • Branch meetings: Monthly
  • Constituency meetings: Quarterly
  • County meetings: Bi-annually
  • National meetings: Annually

Special meetings may be called as needed. Members are notified in advance.

Policies & Positions

Our main policy areas include:

  • Economic Development: Job creation, support for SMEs, and inclusive growth
  • Education: Quality education for all, technical skills development
  • Healthcare: Universal healthcare access and improved infrastructure
  • Governance: Transparency, accountability, and citizen participation
  • Environment: Sustainable development and renewable energy

Read our full manifesto for detailed policy positions.

Members can contribute to policy development through:

  • Participating in policy forums and workshops
  • Submitting policy proposals to relevant committees
  • Engaging in public consultations
  • Joining policy working groups
  • Attending party conventions where policies are debated

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues:

  • Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache
  • Ensure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Check your internet connection
  • Contact our technical support team at tech@kenyagreatparty.org
  • Call our support hotline: 0700 000 001

To reset your password:

  • Go to the login page
  • Click on "Forgot Password"
  • Enter your email address
  • Check your email for reset instructions
  • Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
