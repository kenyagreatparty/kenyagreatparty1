About Kenya Great Party

Building a Brighter
Future for All Kenyans

Kenya Great Party (KGP) is committed to creating a prosperous and united Kenya through visionary leadership and inclusive policies. Our mission is to empower every Kenyan citizen and build a nation we can all be proud of.

  • Promoting sustainable development and job creation

  • Ensuring quality education for all Kenyans

  • Improving access to healthcare nationwide
