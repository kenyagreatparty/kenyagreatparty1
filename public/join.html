Data Privacy Policy
1. Data Collection

We collect personal information including but not limited to:

  • Name, email, phone number, and address
  • National ID number and passport details (optional)
  • Ethnicity, religion, and demographic information
  • Special interest areas and preferences
  • PWD status and NCPWD number (if applicable)
2. Data Usage

Your personal data will be used for:

  • Membership registration and verification
  • Communication regarding party activities
  • Statistical analysis and reporting
  • Compliance with legal requirements
3. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or for legitimate party purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
6. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: privacy@kenyagreatparty.org

Terms and Conditions
1. Membership Eligibility

To become a member of the Kenya Great Party, you must:

  • Be a Kenyan citizen
  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Provide accurate and complete information
  • Agree to abide by party rules and regulations
2. Membership Rights

As a member, you have the right to:

  • Participate in party activities
  • Vote in internal party elections
  • Access party information and resources
  • Contribute to policy development
3. Membership Obligations

As a member, you agree to:

  • Uphold party values and principles
  • Respect other members and party officials
  • Participate constructively in party activities
  • Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information
4. Termination of Membership

Membership may be terminated for:

  • Violation of party rules
  • Conduct unbecoming of a member
  • Resignation by the member
  • Death of the member
5. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from membership will be resolved through the party's internal dispute resolution mechanism.

Membership Agreement
Kenya Great Party Membership Agreement

I, the undersigned, hereby apply for membership in the Kenya Great Party and agree to the following terms:

1. Commitment to Party Values

I commit to upholding the values of democracy, transparency, accountability, and service to the people of Kenya.

2. Active Participation

I agree to actively participate in party activities, contribute to policy development, and support party candidates in elections.

3. Financial Obligations

I understand that membership may involve financial contributions as determined by the party leadership, and I agree to fulfill these obligations.

4. Code of Conduct

I agree to conduct myself in a manner that reflects positively on the party and to treat all members and officials with respect.

5. Confidentiality

I agree to maintain the confidentiality of sensitive party information and not to disclose such information to unauthorized persons.

6. Compliance with Laws

I agree to comply with all applicable laws and regulations in my activities as a party member.

7. Termination

I understand that my membership may be terminated for violation of this agreement or party rules, and I agree to accept such termination.

By signing this agreement, I acknowledge that I have read, understood, and agree to be bound by its terms.
